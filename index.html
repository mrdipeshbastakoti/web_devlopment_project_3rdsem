About Our Department

In a fast-changing digital era, technologies like Artificial Intelligence, IoT, and Cloud Computing are reshaping how we live, learn, and work. As innovation accelerates, intelligent computing has become the driving force behind new solutions and future-ready industries.

Originally established in 2024 for international students, our department evolved from a convergence major into the Department of Intelligence Computing in 2025, building a stronger foundation for academic and career success.

Our curriculum combines solid theoretical knowledge with hands-on learning, industry collaboration, and real project experience. We focus on developing creativity, critical thinking, and practical skills that prepare students for a rapidly changing global tech environment.

Beyond technical excellence, we strive to cultivate innovative leaders who create new value in society. We support our students in discovering their strengths, expanding their perspectives, and advancing confidently toward the future.

Join us and take the next step toward your dream career in the world of intelligent computing!
